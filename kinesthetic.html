Kinesthetic Play

What is Kinesthetic Play?

Rising onto your toes, you begin to twirl in a circle, arms widespread as you see the room shift colors, faster and faster, colors meld into a blur.

As you stop, you feel your feet lead you sideways quickly heading towards the couch. Laying down, eyes open, watching the ceiling as it continues to move. After a few seconds it comes to a stop. You feel the buzzing energy course through your body as you lay there noticing your heavy breathing soften. Relaxed and smiling.

In kinesthetic play we will explore a wide variety of movement play, from running around like airplanes to climbing trees, to moving like different animals. Movement play is wonderful for experiencing pleasant sensations in your body which helps cultivate loving relationships with our bodies. It has been scientifically shown that the benefits of kinesthetic play are increased information retention, improved muscle memory, improved critical thinking and problem-solving skills, and improved self-confidence.

Child-Led Play sessions are held virtually from the comfort of your own home or backyard. You are invited to join this global community of curious adults who chose to venture back in time to their most cherished childhood play activities.
