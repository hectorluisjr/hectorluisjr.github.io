Storytelling Play

What is Storytelling Play?

Going to the book shelf, you see row after row of brightly colored books.

On the covers there are animals, trucks and cars, and mythical creatures. Your hand grazes over the books before pulling your favorite one out. Walking over to the couch with your teddy bear, you sit down and are wrapped in a blanket. A gentle and expressive voice begins to read. You take in the many colors and excitement of the story unfolding. You imagine being a part of the story and memorize each line. You are immersed and present.

We will explore activities like reading your favorite childhood stories, creating your own stories, and singing songs. This practice has the potential to help you write your own life story, tapping into your creativity and expansive imagination. Being read to is also a very calming and nurturing experience if you are craving care. Current data suggests that there are several benefits to storytelling play from improved memory to better mood to better interpersonal relationship.

Child-Led Play sessions are held virtually from the comfort of your own home or backyard. You are invited to join this global community of curious adults who chose to venture back in time to their most cherished childhood play activities.
