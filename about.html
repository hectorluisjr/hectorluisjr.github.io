What is Child-Led Arts?

When was the last time you took your shoes off and wiggled your toes in the mud? When did you last stop to catch bubbles or jump up and down like a frog?

Do you remember the joy you experienced in the simple pleasures these activities brought you as a child? Would you like to feel that again?

This practice and program is for the mavericks, the open-minded, the dreamers, and the curious. Your kid self is very wise. Humans benefit in various ways by connecting to play throughout their lifespan. Who better to connect to play from than a child? Not just any child, but your own inner child who knows what inherently feels fun and natural for you. CLA will help you access your creativity, joy, vibrancy, and connection to the magic of life.

There is always time for play!

Our Promise

I.

This will be a safe container to be as silly as you want with no judgement.

II.

Play is like a muscle, when it stops being used it does not disappear it just weakens. We will meet you where you are at.

III.

This practice is led by your inner knowing of what feels fun to your body ~ you will never have to do anything that does not feel fun and safe to you!

IV.

Engaging in this play practice will be a transformative, loving and delightful experience.

Child-Led Play sessions are held virtually from the comfort of your own home or backyard. You are invited to join this global community of curious adults who chose to venture back in time to their most cherished childhood play activities.
