Sensory Play

What is Sensory Play?

Looking out the window, you see the rain start to fall as the sun still shines through.

In excitement, you run outside barefoot. You feel the wet grass and mud as you give the earth little toe hugs. The crisp refreshing smells of nature fill your nose with every inhale. The rain feels like it is dancing on your skin as you twirl and tilt your head back, listening to gentle thunder in the background.

Sensory play is the engagement of play through our 5 senses and is how we as children were able to experience our environment fully. Both children and adults retain more information when multiple senses are engaged and is a great way to stimulate neuroplasticity. This practice is wonderful for accessing being in the present moment, especially if you have been working all day and feeling stuck in your thoughts.

Child-Led Play sessions are held virtually from the comfort of your own home or backyard. You are invited to join this global community of curious adults who chose to venture back in time to their most cherished childhood play activities.
