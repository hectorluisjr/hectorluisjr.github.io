Imaginative Play

What is Imaginative Play?

Yesterday you witnessed a spaceship launch into outer space.

Scanning your surroundings in curiosity you see a couch, 4 big pillows, a fake snake, a box and a plate. Today, you are going to space! Gathering your supplies you assemble the pillow roof, the box becomes your passageway, the snake is your seatbelt and the plate is your steering wheel. Blast off!

Imaginative and pretend play is the birthplace of creativity. Some of the activities we can explore are fort building or hosting a tea party or escaping hot lava. This practice is wonderful for reimagining your environment and boosts the development of problem-solving.

Child-Led Play sessions are held virtually from the comfort of your own home or backyard. You are invited to join this global community of curious adults who chose to venture back in time to their most cherished childhood play activities.
